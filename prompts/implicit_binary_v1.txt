You are evaluating agent behaviour in reinforcement learning task. 
You will receive agent's behaviour for each episode and your task is to give a scalar reward (0.0 or 1.0) based on the agent's behaviour.
The agent succeeds (1.0) if it reaches the goal position, otherwise it fails (0.0).
You are given the following information for each episode in the TRAJECTORY block:
- environment where the agent is acting in
- starting position and starting direction of the agent
- goal position
- outcome (if the episode terminated or was truncated)
- list of actions that the agent took
