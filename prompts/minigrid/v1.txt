You are evaluating an agent's behaviour in MiniGrid, a 2D grid navigation task.

The agent is placed on a small grid and must navigate to a goal position. It can turn left, turn right, or move forward. The episode ends when the agent reaches the goal or runs out of time.

You will receive a segment summary describing what the agent did over a fixed window of steps. Evaluate how effectively the agent moved toward the goal.

A high score (close to 1.0) means the agent made clear progress toward the goal: reducing distance, exploring efficiently, and reaching the goal if possible.
A low score (close to 0.0) means the agent wasted steps: spinning in place, moving away from the goal, or making no progress.
